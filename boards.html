UAT Major Objectives

Boards

Six program objectives for the AI & Data Science major from the UAT catalog. These outcomes guide the skills and knowledge developed throughout the degree and will be demonstrated in SIP411.

Artificial Intelligence — UAT Catalog

AI Program Objectives

Six learning objectives from the AI track of the AI & Data Science major.

01

Cognitive AI Integration

Create and integrate cognitive artificial intelligence into applications.

02

Natural Language Processing

Create and evolve natural language processing systems.

03

Machine Learning Systems

Demonstrate proficiency in designing and developing machine learning systems using industry approaches and patterns.

04

Deep Learning

Design and employ deep learning systems using best practices and conventions.

05

Neural Networks

Demonstrate a deep understanding of neural networks and their role in creating intelligent systems.

06

Advanced AI Systems

Design, create, or integrate reinforcement learning, large language models, and genetic algorithms in intelligent software.

Data Science — UAT Catalog

Data Science Program Objectives

Six learning objectives from the Data Science track of the AI & Data Science major.

01

Large-Scale Data Management

Assess, structure, manage and implement large data sets derived from real-time sources.

02

Data Compilation & Engineering

Compile data from multiple sources, including data selection, data scrubbing and feature engineering, with an emphasis on tidy data and the tidyverse.

03

Statistical Analysis

Apply statistical tests and tools appropriately to analyze data sets drawn from different types of sources (nature, humankind, organizations, etc.), making inferences and projections from the data.

04

Data Visualization

Create visualizations of large data sets in ways that clarify understanding of their meaning and implications.

05

AI & Statistical Solutions

Design and implement big data, artificial intelligence and statistical and visual analysis solutions that provide people and organizations with understanding, guidance and options drawn from the data.

06

Ethics & Digital Privacy

Demonstrate best practices regarding digital privacy and the ethical use of personal information.
