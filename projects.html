RouteCart

Student Innovation Project — In Progress

View on GitHub →

A grocery trip optimization mobile app that takes a user's shopping list, identifies nearby stores, normalizes unit prices and package sizes, estimates travel cost and trip time, and returns three ranked plans: cheapest, fastest, and best value. RouteCart answers the question every budget-conscious shopper faces — is it actually worth driving to two stores?
